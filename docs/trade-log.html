Why The Trade Log Matters

The trade log makes the backtest auditable. It shows:

  • when entries and exits occurred
  • how long trades stayed open
  • whether exits came from success, stop-loss, timeout, or breakout failure
  • how profits and losses were distributed across trades
  • how the final ML overlay changes position sizing on top of those same breakout opportunities
